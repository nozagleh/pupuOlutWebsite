Never forget

how it tastes like

Standing in the store looking at beers but can't remember what it tasted like? This solves all of your problems so you don't have to remember it on your own. Add drinks with uniqe names, description and star rating. Search your drink list by name, rating and type of the drink.

No fuzz

Clean navigation

Easy and clean navigation that is nice to use. The main page has two buttons for Adding and Finding drinks in the database. Adding beer should be easy at any pint in time with simple inputs and star rating. Finding drinks is extremely easy with the three taps, these taps provide searching for name, type and rating by stars. Tapping on a drink brings up further information about the drinks and gives and edit option. Long tapping on a drink deletes the drink from the list.
